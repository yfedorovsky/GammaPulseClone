# MU FQ3 earnings setup (pre-registered 2026-06-20)

Written **before** the June 24 print so the read can't become hindsight. The structural
super-cycle is real and multi-year; this note is about the **near-term binary**, where the
honest verdict is the opposite of the prevailing X/Reddit euphoria.

## The catalyst (facts)
- **Wed June 24 2026, after close** (~4:30pm ET call). Fiscal Q3.
- Company guide: revenue **~$33.5B** (±$0.75B), gross margin **~81%**, EPS **$19.15** (±$0.40).
  Street consensus EPS ~**$20.57**. Analyst revenue range **$33.7B–$40.9B** — a >$7B span =
  genuine uncertainty on the ramp pace.
- HBM "sold out for several quarters"; HBM4 12-Hi shipping, 16-Hi sampled. Prior quarter:
  record $6.9B FCF, dividend +30%, order books into 2027.

## Why this is a CROWDED, priced-for-perfection binary (the non-consensus caution)
- **Bull case is consensus**: "Wall Street unanimously bullish," GM maybe breaking 80%, sold-out HBM.
- **Sentiment at euphoric extremes**: Reddit sentiment ~85, "gains-porn + trillion-dollar-milestone
  threads dominating — exactly the retail euphoria that marked prior memory tops." Stock has run
  hard and trades **above** the average analyst price target.
- **Elevated options IV** into the event → IV-crush + sell-the-news risk **even on a beat**.
- The **$3T cumulative-OP** forecast circulating (Wall St Engine post) is ~5× the defensible
  super-cycle range — sentiment marker, not a number to size against (see fact-check note).
- Net: asymmetry into the print is **poor for naive longs** — a beat is largely priced; any
  blemish gaps it down. **The edge, if any, is in the post-print reaction once IV collapses and
  the 2027 tone is known — not in front-running the crowd.**

## Pre-registered TELLS TO WATCH (the real non-consensus signals — judge the call on these, not the headline)
1. **HBM4 2027 allocation + pricing tone** — visibility/firmness into 2027 is the whole thesis.
   Caution here = the sell-the-news trigger.
2. **Samsung HBM4 catch-up** — Samsung passed NVDA/AMD quals (+40–50% logic-die pricing) but yield
   reportedly **<60% vs SK Hynix ~80%**; SK Hynix ~70% of NVDA Rubin HBM4 (UBS). Any sign Samsung's
   yield is closing = supply-response / pricing-pressure risk into late 2027.
3. **NAND / data-center bit growth** — the UNDER-watched broadening (Jukan: NAND export $/kg
   +546% Y/Y, **+28% M/M**; DC bits >50% of TAM). Market is fixated on HBM; NAND strength is the
   secondary positive most aren't pricing.
4. **Hyperscaler capex-digestion language** — any hint of demand pacing/digestion.
5. **Mix vs price honesty** — the SK export $/kg surges (+576% DRAM) overstate pure ASP via mix
   enrichment; watch whether mgmt frames pricing as durable contract ASP vs mix/HBM-shift.

## Post-print playbook (analysis of what each outcome IMPLIES — not a directive)
- **Beat + constructive, firm 2027 HBM4 tone + no Samsung-yield concern** → super-cycle "real not
  cresting"; memory-complex strength can continue (MU + NAND read-through to SNDK).
- **Beat but cautious 2027 / Samsung-catch-up acknowledged / capex-digestion language** → the
  priced-in story disappoints; rotation / profit-taking risk despite a "good" quarter.
- **In all cases, IV collapses post-print** — pre-print directional longs pay the vol premium; the
  cleaner read is the reaction to the specific guidance, after the crush.
