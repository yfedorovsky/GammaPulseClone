## What option-flow research says and what it does not say

The option-flow evidence is useful, but less clean than the underlying-order-flow evidence. Older intraday stock-option work finds that **stock** net-buy volume has strong predictive power for subsequent stock and option returns, while **call or put net-buy volume alone** has little predictive ability.
